# Trading System Workflow Summary

## System Overview

This trading system is based on **Bollinger Bands + SuperTrend + RAVI + RSI**, using differentiated trading strategies for different market states (range/trend).

**Core Philosophy**: First identify trend, then trade; cut losses short, let profits run; capital never idle.

## Entry Conditions Comparison

### Range Market Entry

| Condition | Long                              | Short                             |
|-----------|-----------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Bollinger | close < lower_band                | close > upper_band                |
| RSI       | RSI < 30 or Bullish Divergence    | RSI > 70 or Bearish Divergence    |
| Volume    | VolRatio < 0.5                    | VolRatio < 0.5                    |

### Trend Market Entry

| Condition | Long on Pullback                             | Short on Rebound                            |
|-----------|----------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| SuperTrend| low <= ST_Long and close > ST_Long           | high >= ST_Short and close < ST_Short       |
| RSI       | RSI > 50 and crossing up 50                  | RSI < 50 and crossing down 50               |
| Volume    | VolRatio < 0.8                               | VolRatio < 0.8                              |

## Exit Rules

### Stop Loss Setup

```
- Long Stop:  entry_price - stop_loss_mult * ATR
- Short Stop: entry_price + stop_loss_mult * ATR
- Range Market: stop_loss_mult = 1.5
- Trend Market: stop_loss_mult = 2.0
```

### Profit Taking / Position Reduction

| Market Type | First Reduction                        | Second Reduction                          |
|-------------|----------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------|
| Range       | Profit >= 1.5*ATR, reduce 50%          | Profit >= 2.5*ATR, reduce remaining 50%   |
| Trend       | Profit >= 2*ATR, reduce 30%            | Profit >= 4*ATR, reduce remaining 50%     |

### Time Stop

- Range Market: Hold >= 12 K-lines and floating profit < 0.5*ATR -> Close
- Trend Market: Hold >= 24 K-lines and floating profit < 0.5*ATR -> Close

### Trailing Stop Activation

When floating profit >= 1*ATR, move stop loss to break-even price, then follow SuperTrend line.

---

## Money Management

```
Risk Amount per Trade = Total Account * Risk Ratio (default 1%)
Position Size = Risk Amount / Stop Loss Distance
3 Consecutive Losses -> Risk Ratio halved
Max Drawdown >= 15% -> Pause Trading
```

## Key Summary Points

1. **Trend First**: Identify market state (range/trend) first, then decide trading strategy
2. **Multi-Dimension Confirmation**: System B uses multi-timeframe filtering to improve trade quality
3. **Strict Stop Loss**: Range 1.5*ATR, Trend 2.0*ATR
4. **Dynamic Management**: Trailing stop + staged profit taking, let profits run
5. **Risk Control**: 1% risk per trade, halve position after 3 consecutive losses
